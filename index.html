Onff  line Mismatch

Identify and Protect Popular Local Business
in Gentrifying Neighborhoods

Current trend of gentrification induced by rising rent in NYC has laid a huge burden on local small business owners, and in turn, it impacts the overall urban life diversity. OtoO Mismatch uses the NYC Chinatown area as a case-study and demonstrate the local businesses that are crucial to the community daily experience. It uses anonymous location data to identify most visited stores by local communities and compare these stores’ presence on Yelp to construct an “OtoO (online vs. offline) mismatch index”.
